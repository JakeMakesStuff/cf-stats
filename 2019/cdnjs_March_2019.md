# cdnjs March 2019 Usage Stats
Information provided directly by Cloudflare for the `cdnjs.cloudflare.com` domain. ⛅️

## Key Highlights
 - cdnjs served **just under 160 billion requests** in March.
 - During the month we used **over 2.5 petabytes of bandwidth**.
 - That is over **5 billion requests and 83 terabytes of data per day**.
 
### Library Highlights
 - jQuery (3.3.1) was the top library with nearly 4 billion requests for the single file.
 - FontAwesome (4.7.0) is also near the top with over 6 billion combined requests.
 - The CookieConsent library (3.0.3) makes an appearance near the top with just under 4 billion combined requests.

## Total Number of Requests
> The first important stat for us is the total number of requests sent to cdnjs.cloudflare.com.\
> Cloudflare provides this number to us at a 1% sample for the whole month, giving 1,599,732,352 at 1%.
> 
> Recalculated to be a rough estimate for the entire month, this is 159,973,235,200.

**Just under 160 billion requests this month or just over 5 billion requests each day**. 📈

## Total Bandwidth Usage
> Another great stat that Cloudflare have given us this month is the bandwidth usage for the cdnjs.cloudflare.com domain.\
> Once again, this number is provided at a 1% sample for the month and in gigabytes.
> 
> Bringing this up to 100% (roughly), this would give us 2,587,711 GB of bandwidth used.

**That is a huge 2.5 petabytes of bandwidth used for cdnjs requests in March**. 🤯
